# Repository Collaboration Rules

## Daily CUDA Interactive Q&A Mode

When the user says they want to study CUDA through interactive questions:

1. Ask 10 concept-understanding questions, one at a time.
2. Wait for the user's answer before moving to the next question.
3. After each answer, briefly review it:
   - confirm what is correct
   - correct misunderstandings
   - add one concise explanation or example
4. Keep the session lightweight and avoid overloading the user.
5. After the 10th question, summarize:
   - concepts understood
   - mistakes or weak points
   - suggested next topics
6. If the user asks to save the session, save it under `days/dayXXX_topic_name/README.md`.
7. The saved record should include:
   - date
   - topic
   - 10 questions
   - explanations
   - correct answers
   - summary
8. Do not save the user's original answers in the daily record.

## 每日 CUDA 交互问答学习模式

当用户说想通过交互问答学习 CUDA 时：

1. 一次只出 1 道题，总共 10 道概念理解题。
2. 等用户回答后，再进入下一题。
3. 每次回答后先简短点评：
   - 确认正确部分
   - 修正错误理解
   - 补充一个简短解释或例子
4. 保持轻量，不一次性灌太多内容。
5. 第 10 题结束后，总结：
   - 已掌握概念
   - 易错点
   - 下一步建议
6. 如果用户要求保存学习记录，保存到 `days/dayXXX_topic_name/README.md`。
7. 保存内容包括：
   - 日期
   - 主题
   - 10 个问题
   - 解说
   - 正确答案
   - 总结
8. 每日记录中不保存用户的原始回答。
